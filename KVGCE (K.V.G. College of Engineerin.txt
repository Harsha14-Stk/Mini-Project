KVGCE (K.V.G. College of Engineering) is a private engineering college in Sullia, Dakshina Kannada, Karnataka, founded in 1986. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs and focuses on providing quality technical education, encouraging innovation, and preparing students for industry needs and entrepreneurial skills. The college is known for its research activities, experienced faculty, and focus on holistic student development.
